Top 5 Best Pancake Restaurants in Toronto on Budget!

Best Pancakes In Toronto

We all know as students we're always trying to save money and find the best possible deals.

Here are the Best Pancakes in Toronto if you're on a Budget!


The following Pancake restaurants were judged from the following:

  1. Taste
  2. Price
  3. Ambience

5.Emma's Country Kitchen

Emma's Country Kitchen is located at 255 St.Clair West. The pancakes are delious priced at $15.


Emma's Country Kitchen

4. Mildred Temple Kitchen

Hands down one of the best pancake shots in toronto that's worth a try. The pancakes are priced at $14.


3. Denny's

Denny's is located just 2 minute walk from Yonge & Dundas Square. The pancakes are priced at $11.

2. Aunties & Uncles Diner

Take it back old school at this cute diner located in Little Italy. The pancakes are priced at $9.

1. Okonomi House Restaurant

Looking for a bit of sweet and savory pancakes? Well these would be your best bet. Japanese style pancakes are priced at $7.
